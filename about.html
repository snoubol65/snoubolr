About

We are building AI tools for early-stage startup development.

Snoubolr is an early-stage platform concept focused on helping builders analyze their projects, find similar startups, connect with relevant founders, and use community feedback to improve product direction.

Problem

Most early founders lack relevant feedback, clear product direction, and access to people building similar ideas. Existing communities are noisy and rarely provide project-specific analytics.

Solution

Snoubolr uses AI to analyze project information, identify useful development signals, and connect founders with similar startups, collaborators, and communities.
