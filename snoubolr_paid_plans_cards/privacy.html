Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026. This policy explains how Snoubolr handles information during private beta.

Information we collect

We may collect contact information, company or project information, beta request details, website usage data, and communications sent to admin@snoubolr.online. We do not ask users to submit government IDs, payment card numbers, or sensitive personal documents through the website.

How we use information

We use information to operate the private beta, evaluate product fit, generate startup analytics, provide support, improve our platform, and communicate with users.

AI processing

Project descriptions and business notes submitted by users may be processed by AI systems to generate summaries, scores, classifications, and recommendations.

Retention

We keep beta information only as long as needed for product development, support, and legal obligations. Users may request deletion by emailing admin@snoubolr.online.
