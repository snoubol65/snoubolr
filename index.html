What Snoubolr does

We use AI to understand projects and help founders move forward.

Snoubolr is designed as an AI layer for startup development. It reads project profiles, extracts key signals, compares similar ideas, and helps founders understand where their project fits and who they should connect with.

AI analytics engine

From startup profiles to clear project signals.

Our vision is to use AI to transform unstructured founder input into structured project intelligence. This helps the platform recommend similar startups, detect collaboration opportunities, and generate useful direction for project development.

  • Analyze startup descriptions, goals, markets, and user problems.
  • Detect similarities between projects even when wording is different.
  • Suggest communities, founders, and projects with high relevance.
  • Turn feedback and engagement into measurable growth signals.
